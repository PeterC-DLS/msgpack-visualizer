MessagePack Visualizer

Created by sugendran in an attempt to find out why things are breaking. Forked by dbrgn to improve a few things.

You can initialize the visualizer with data by using the #base64=<data>, #array=<data> or #hexarray=<data> hashes in the URL.

For a full list of features, refer to the README. When in doubt, please refer to the msgpack spec.
